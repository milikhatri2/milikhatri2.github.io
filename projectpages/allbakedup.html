About Project

As a researcher for the All Baked Up project, I collaborated with two team members to examine the relationship between visual aesthetics and user engagement in bakery websites. Our research aimed to understand how design elements such as color, typography, and layout influence users’ perceptions and interactions.

  • All Baked Up UX Research Report
  • All Baked Up UX Research Presentation

Outcome

  • Impact & Outcomes: Our research confirmed that visually appealing bakery websites significantly enhance user engagement.
  • Design Insights: We identified best practices for bakery website aesthetics, including clear menu structures and authentic imagery.
  • Practical Applications: These insights help small businesses optimize their online presence.
