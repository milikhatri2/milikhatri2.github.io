Summary

Led design and prototyping for a digital currency app focused on user needs and security. Created wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma, iterating based on team feedback.

Year

2023

About Project

As part of a dynamic team of five, I took on the role of the designer and prototyper for an innovative digital currency app. This project was undertaken as part of a class assignment, focusing on addressing specific user needs within the financial technology sector.

  • Figma: Riva Figma Prototype
  • User-Centered Design: By focusing on the needs and pain points of our personas, I ensured the app’s design was user-centric and addressed real-world scenarios.
  • Interactive Prototypes: My prototypes provided a realistic representation of the app, helping stakeholders and team members understand the end product's look and feel.
  • Collaborative Effort: I worked closely with my team, incorporating feedback and iterating on designs to refine the app continuously.

The final prototype of our digital currency app was well-received, showcasing a seamless user experience with robust security features. The project not only demonstrated my design and prototyping skills but also my ability to collaborate effectively within a team to deliver a compelling digital solution.

My Role

As the designer and prototyper, my responsibilities included:

  • Wireframing: I created initial wireframes to outline the app’s structure and flow. These wireframes served as the foundation for our design process, ensuring all team members were aligned on the app’s functionality and user experience.
  • Prototyping in Figma: Leveraging Figma, I transformed wireframes into high-fidelity interactive prototypes. This allowed us to visualize the app’s design and user interactions, facilitating user testing and iterative improvements.
