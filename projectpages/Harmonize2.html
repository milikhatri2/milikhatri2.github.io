UX Case Study

Harmonize

UI Design Figma Prototyping Team Collaboration EdTech

Problem Statement

Many beginners struggle to stay motivated when learning music through traditional methods, which often feel repetitive and hard to sustain. Harmonize addresses this challenge by blending music education with gamified experiences that help users stay consistent, engaged, and motivated.

Objectives & Goals

  • Design a mobile app that makes learning music engaging and rewarding. Transform traditional music lessons into an interactive, enjoyable experience.
  • Use gamification to boost user motivation and consistency. Incorporate levels, rewards, and challenges to keep users engaged over time.
  • Create an intuitive, beginner-friendly interface. Ensure smooth navigation and an easy learning curve for users of all skill levels.
  • Capture a growing market of digital learners. Attract users seeking fun, accessible ways to learn music through technology.

Our Process

Discover

Conducted competitive analysis of 5 music-learning and gamified education apps.

Define

Ran surveys and interviews with 8 beginner and intermediate music learners to identify pain points.

Ideate

Mapped out key user journeys and wireframed core features around progress tracking and bite-sized lessons.

Design

Developed a high-fidelity prototype in Figma showcasing onboarding, lessons, and gamified progress features.

User Needs

  • Interactive, non-repetitive exercises that maintain motivation - Users need varied, engaging exercises that provide immediate feedback to keep practice interesting and prevent boredom.
  • Short, flexible lessons suited for busy schedules - Users need bite-sized lessons that can fit into their daily routines, making consistent practice more achievable.
  • Clear visual indicators of progress (levels, points, achievements) - Users need visible milestones and rewards that track improvement and encourage continued learning.
  • A fun and rewarding learning experience that feels like a game - Users need a playful, game-like environment that makes learning enjoyable and motivates regular practice.

Outcome

The final result was a high-fidelity Figma prototype that demonstrated Harmonize’s complete user journey - from onboarding to lesson completion and progress tracking. In usability testing with 6 users, participants found the interface “intuitive” and “motivating,” with 5 out of 6 noting that gamified rewards encouraged continued use. The project emphasized the value of user-centered design and the importance of grounding creative decisions in research. Future opportunities include adding social features, adaptive lessons, and partnerships with educators.
